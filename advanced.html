13.3 Stylistic change

The question of historical change is intricably related to musical styles. Here, we adopt the framework for style analysis layed out by Meyer (1989). In particular, the adaptation of Jan (2016) and its schematic representation is already almost a description of a statistical graphical model.

  • “Statistical Evolutionary Laws in Music Styles” (Nakamura & Kaneko, 2019)
  • “Investigating style evolution of Western classical music: A computational approach” (Weiß et al., 2019)
